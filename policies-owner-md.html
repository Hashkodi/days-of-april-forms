What it should feel like

13In your own words: when a good new employee has been here three months, what do you want them to say this company is like?

This becomes the opening of your handbook, in your voice; without it we would be guessing at the most personal page in the book.

14Name up to three things people here should always DO, whatever their job. Actions, not qualities: "we call the customer back the same day" rather than "we are professional".

These become the handbook's "how we behave here" page: rules of the house your managers can actually point to, teach and praise.

14bWould you like to offer paternity leave? There is currently no legal requirement in the private sector; some companies grant three to five days.

For comparison, mothers are entitled by law to paid maternity leave for every birth. A few days for fathers is a low-cost benefit staff talk about warmly. We will put a recommendation in the report either way.
