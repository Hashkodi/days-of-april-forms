Where your paperwork stands

Prepared for your company · for the owner and whoever runs HR

This short form tells us where your paperwork stands today, so the kit fixes what is actually missing and leaves alone what already works. Nothing here is scored, and gaps are exactly what the kit is for. If the same person runs the business and the HR side, answer everything.

A quick note before you start. This is not about finding fault with anyone, and it is certainly not a test of the person who looks after HR. Most companies your size have some paperwork behind, for the simple reason that the work grew faster than the admin. Our only aim is to get your HR tidy and your company on the right side of the rules, with as little disruption to you as possible, so that none of this can come back to bite the business later. The more openly you answer, the less you will have to think about it again.
