Prepared for your company · for the owner / managing director

This short form helps us build your onboarding kit around how your company actually works, not a generic model. This is the owner's half, and it takes only a few minutes. Your answers set the direction, so the kit fixes the things you actually want fixed.

Nothing here is a test of anyone, and it is not about finding fault. It is here to help you and the company get onboarding streamlined and keep you clear of trouble later, so gaps are exactly what the kit is for and the honest answer is always the useful one.

01

Your hiring, in numbers

M1Roughly how many people do you expect to hire over the next 12 months?

The kit is sized to your real hiring pace: three joiners a year need a lighter process than thirty.

M2Of the people who joined in the last year, how many have already left?

This number tells us how urgent the fix is, and gives you a before-and-after measure once the kit is running.

02

What a good start looks like

M3Think of a role you hire for often. By the end of month three, what should that person be doing without help?

Your answer becomes the 90-day target in the probation plan, so new people are measured against what the business actually needs.

M4Is there anything about how people join the company that you already know you want fixed?

We build that fix into the kit first, so you see value from the very first new joiner.

03

Staying in the loop

M5Would you like to receive the new joiners' month-three feedback summaries yourself, alongside HR? (Recommended.)

Early leavers decide early. Owners who see this feedback directly catch a bad start while it can still be fixed.
