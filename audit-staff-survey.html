04

Voice and communication

I hear company news from management before I hear it as rumour.

If I had a serious complaint, I know exactly where to take it besides my own manager.

How it feels to work here

Evenings and weekends becoming work time is the exception here, not the habit.

Bad behaviour gets dealt with here, whoever does it.

The company's stated values match how people actually behave.

Managers who people leave because of are noticed and dealt with.

My manager talks to me one-to-one on some regular rhythm.
