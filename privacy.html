Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

This policy covers April Hour Publisher, the application April Hour · AI Agents (operated by Days of April (Private) Limited, Sri Lanka) uses to run its own presence on Instagram: publishing our posts, and reading and answering comments and direct messages sent to our account, @aprilhouraiagents.

What we collect

When you comment on our posts or message our Instagram account, we receive what Instagram provides to page tools: your username, and the text of your comment or message. If you fill one of our forms (for example the AI Readiness Check), we receive the answers you type and the contact details you choose to give.

How we use it

  • To answer your comment or message, sometimes with an automated first reply, always with a person behind it for anything the automation cannot answer.
  • To follow up on genuine enquiries about our services.
  • Nothing else. We do not sell your information, use it for advertising, or share it with anyone beyond the service providers that run our tools (Meta, Cloudflare, GitHub, Notion, Google Workspace).

How long we keep it

Enquiry records are kept while we are in conversation with you and for up to two years after, then deleted. Message-processing records used to avoid answering the same message twice are deleted automatically within 24 hours.

Your choices

You can ask us at any time to see what we hold about you, or to delete it. Write to aprilhouraiagents@gmail.com and we will act on the request within seven days. You can also delete your side of any Instagram conversation through Instagram itself.
