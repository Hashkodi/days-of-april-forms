Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

This policy covers Days of April Publisher, the application Days of April · HR Consultancy uses to run its own presence on Instagram: publishing our posts, and reading and answering comments and direct messages sent to our account, @daysofaprilhr. It works only on our own account and does not read or touch anyone else's account.

What we collect

When you comment on our posts or message our Instagram account, we receive what Instagram provides to page tools: your username, and the text of your comment or message. That is the full list. The app collects nothing else: no contact lists, no browsing data, and no information about your account beyond the message you chose to send us.

How we use it

  • To answer your message, sometimes with an automated first reply, always with a real person behind it for anything the automation cannot answer.
  • To follow up on genuine enquiries about our HR consulting services.
  • Nothing else. We never sell your information, never use it for advertising, and never share it with anyone beyond the service providers that run our tools (Meta, Cloudflare, GitHub, Notion, Google Workspace).

How long we keep it

Enquiry records are kept while we are in conversation with you and for up to two years after, then deleted. Message-processing records used to avoid answering the same message twice are deleted automatically within 24 hours.

We never message you first

The app only ever replies to a message or comment you send us. It does not send unsolicited messages, and it does not add you to any list.

Your choices

You can ask us at any time to see what we hold about you, or to delete it. Write to daysofaprilhr@gmail.com or send us a DM, and we will act on the request within seven days. You can also delete your side of any Instagram conversation through Instagram itself.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version is published at this address with a new date at the top.
