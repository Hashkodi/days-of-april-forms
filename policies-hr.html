This form tells us which documents your company actually needs, so we draft only those, and it maps how your company runs, so every page describes you rather than a template. Where something does not exist yet, saying so is exactly what we need, because that is what we build for you. It takes about ten minutes, and you can send it in one sitting.

This is not here to find fault with you or anyone else. It is here to help the company set out plainly how things are done, so nothing is left to guesswork later. Where a question does not apply, say so and move on.
